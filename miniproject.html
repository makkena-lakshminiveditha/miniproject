Kids Rhyme Books:

Rhyming encourages children to become active listeners, critical thinkers, and proficient speakers. These skills are fundamental to reading success. As children progress from recognizing rhymes to reading simple books, they carry these foundational skills with them, giving them a head start in the literacy journey.
